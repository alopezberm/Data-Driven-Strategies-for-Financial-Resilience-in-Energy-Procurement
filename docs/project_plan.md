# Project Plan

## Objective
Build a DSS to optimize energy procurement under uncertainty.

## Problem
- High volatility in electricity prices
- Exposure to tail risks
- Static strategies are inefficient

## Approach
- Quantile forecasting
- Decision engine (heuristic + RL)
- Backtesting simulation

## Expected Deliverables
- Forecasting models
- Policy engine
- Backtesting results
- Visual insights

## Timeline (high-level)
- Week 1: Data & EDA
- Week 2: Modeling
- Week 3: Decision + Backtesting
- Week 4: Reporting
